Terrarium v2

A self-contained world with a three-tier food chain: a living meadow, soft motes that graze it, and hot-coloured hunters that chase and eat the motes. A mote has two ways to survive a hunter, and its genes pick one — fleers (fast, keen) outrun the danger in the open, while hiders (small, slow) freeze and vanish into dense vegetation. Each mote wears a halo of its lifestyle: leaf-green for a hider, amber for a fleer. The regime readout names which way the world has tipped — a predator arms-race or a grazer-haven collapse — and a banner marks the moment it flips; the meadow's whole light leans with it, warm and tense in an arms-race, cold and hollow in a grazer-haven. Toggle the overlay (or press O) to reveal the hidden fertility map and where the herd has been grazing.
